Natural Gas Customers Analysis

This document contains information about natural gas customers and their usage patterns. The data includes customer demographics, consumption patterns, and billing information.

Key findings:
- Customer base has grown by 15% this quarter
- Average consumption per customer has increased by 8%
- Industrial customers represent 60% of total revenue
- Residential customers show seasonal variation in usage

Customer Categories:
1. Industrial customers - High volume, stable usage
2. Commercial customers - Medium volume, business hours pattern
3. Residential customers - Low volume, seasonal variation

Market Analysis:
The natural gas market shows strong growth potential with increasing demand from both industrial and residential sectors. Price stability and reliable supply chains are key factors for customer retention.

Recommendations:
- Focus on industrial customer retention programs
- Develop seasonal pricing strategies for residential customers
- Expand commercial customer base through targeted marketing
- Invest in infrastructure to support growing demand
