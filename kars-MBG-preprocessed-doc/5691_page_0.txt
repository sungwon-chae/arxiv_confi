Mercedes-Benz Korea won two awards for Car of the Year 2020 with the new EQC - The new EQC won the Green Car of the Year in 2020 granted by Korea Automobile Journalists Association and the Future Mobility of the Year in 2020 granted by JoongAng Ilbo- The new EQC, the first full electric vehicle under the EQ brand available from last October, has drawn attention as an eco-friendly vehicle with a distinct look and an all-new electric powertrain that delivers a dynamic performance and efficiency(Seoul, March 17, 2020) ? Mercedes-Benz Korea today announced that the new Mercedes-Benz EQC has received two awards for the Car of the Year 2020 from the Korea Automobile Journalists Association and JoongAng Ilbo in the respective categories of the Green Car of the Year and the Future Mobility of the Year.The Car of the Year, launched by the Korea Automobile Journalists Association in 2011, is an annual celebration of the most valuable one among newly introduced cars for the previous one year by assessing them with 10 criteria, such as design, performance and quality, and safety and convenience. After three stages of evaluation, a panel of automobile journalists from 54 media outlets has selected the new EQC as the Green Car of the Year, which is awarded to a new eco-friendly vehicle featuring the most remarkable automotive technology among electric, hybrid and fuel-cell vehicles.2020 JoongAng Ilbo Car of the Year Awards, which celebrates the 11th anniversary, has recently changed the classification of awards for higher prestige and fairness. It has elevated the Future Mobility of the Year to the main award given the growing interest in future mobility across the society and added utility and ADAS to the list of awards in order to reflect the latest trend in the society. It has conducted a detailed Q&A session for 17 new cars in the first round and a test drive evaluation in the next place. Then, the new EQC has been selected as the winner of the Future Mobility of the Year in the fierce competition.The new EQC was launched in Korea last October as the first full electric vehicle under the EQ brand. Based on the powerful and efficient electric powertrain, it combines dynamic driving performance, unique design, cutting-edge digitalization and connectivity technology. In other words, electric intelligence by Mercedes-Benz.The new EQC is available to drive more than 309 km on a single charge, and its battery can be charged to 80% in around 40 minutes with 110kW quick charging.Along with the launch of the new EQC, Mercedes-Benz Korea has introduced a premium charging solution that can help customers to enjoy the performance of the electric vehicle without any difficulties in charging.Mercedes-Benz Korea offers ?EQ Smart Coaching Service, a comprehensive EV charging consulting service by assigning a smart coach for individual EQC customers and providing one-on-one charging consulting service and the Mercedes me Charge Membership Card, which allows customers to top off their cards and pay for charging at most public charging stations.?The new EQC embodies the future mobility strategies of Mercedes-Benz featuring the dynamic performance of the newly-developed electric drivetrain as well as the innovative technology in the connected car.? said Sang-Kuk Lee, the Vice President of Sales at Mercedes-Benz Korea. ?We are honored to have the new EQC fully recognized as the leading model in the green car market. We will be committed to developing the green car segment in Korean market by introducing many more electric models this year.?Meanwhile, along with the new EQC, the new Mercedes-AMG GT 4-Door Coup? also won three awards: the Import Car of the Year in 2020 by JoongAng Ilbo and the Performance Car of the Year in 2020 by the Korea Automobile Journalists Association and the Automobile Writers Association of Korea respectively.# # #For Press Inquiries Yun-ju Hwang, Mercedes-Benz Korea: 02-6456-2598, yun-ju.hwang@daimler.comJieun Song, Mercedes-Benz Korea: 02-6456-2564, jieun.song@daimler.comEunkyung Lee, PRGATE: 02-792-2633, 010-2978-4054, summer@prgate.comEunha Jeong, PRGATE: 02-792-2633, 010-3039-8432, eunha.jeong@prgate.comTaekyeum Lee, PRGATE: 02-792-2633, 010-2089-3899, tk.lee@prgate.comAbout Mercedes-Benz KoreaMercedes-Benz Korea Ltd.
