Surround Sound System, and Climatised front seats as standard- The new EQC is eligible for the government?s subsidy program for low-emission vehicles, offering attractive incentives for customers. (Seoul, June 30, 2020) ? Mercedes-Benz Korea(President & CEO Dimitris Psillakis) announced on June 30 that the company unveils ?The new EQC 400 4MATIC Premium? model.The new EQC is the first full electric vehicle under the EQ, electric vehicle and technology brand of Mercedes-Benz, and embodies the future mobility strategy of Daimler AG. The EQC was first introduced in the Korean market last October with ?The new EQC 400 4MATIC? and ?The new EQC 400 4MATIC edition 1886? models. The new EQC 400 4MATIC Premium model offers more convenience with the addition of the Head Up Display and Climatised front seats as standard features, while boasting a more luxurious interior with leather seats. In addition, the new EQC 400 4MATIC Premium includes the Burmester? Surround Sound System, which was co-developed alongside high-end audio system brand Burmester?, enabling passengers to enjoy a theater-like experience inside the vehicle thanks to its top-of-the-line sound quality.Featuring a combination of a dynamic driving performance with its powerful and efficient electric powertrain, a distinctive design, and the latest driving assistance system, digitalization and connectivity, the new EQC demonstrates the exclusive ?electric intelligence? from Mercedes-Benz. In particular, the latest MBUX (Mercedes-Benz User Experience) infotainment system applied to the new EQC includes exclusive functions for electric vehicles such as state of charge, energy flow, and more. Using the natural speech recognition of MBUX, drivers can say ?Get ready to leave at 8 a.m. tomorrow,? and ?Charge the vehicle to 85%,? to adjust charging settings, designate a comfortable interior temperature, prepare navigation routes, and even set charging and departure times. In addition, Mercedes-Benz Korea has offered a premium charging solution to help customers enjoy the EQC without facing difficulties in regards to vehicle charging. Mercedes-Benz Korea has provided the ?EQ Smart Coaching Service, a comprehensive EV charging consulting service that assigns a smart coach for individual EQC customers to provide one-on-one consulting on charging. Smart coaches also visit and inspect the charging environment of each EQC customers to offer either free installation of a home charger or an unlimited charging card valid for one year at public charging stations if the home charger installation is not possible. When customer use public charging station, a Mercedes me Charge Membership Card makes charging and paying convenient. By simple tag of Mercedes me Charge Membership Card, customers are able to charge their vehicles and pay for the charging at most public charging stations. In addition, the new EQC customers and Mercedes me Charge Membership Card holders can charge their vehicles for free at the Mercedes-Benz charging zone at the 2nd basement-level of the Lotte World Tower until December 2020.The price of the new EQC is KRW 95,500,000 for the EQC 400 4MATIC and KRW 101,400,000 for the EQC 400 4MATIC edition 1886 and the EQC 400 4MATIC Premium, with VAT included. Technical specifications are described below.Meanwhile, the new EQC is now eligible for the government?s subsidy program for low-emission vehicles, offering attractive incentives for customers. The total subsidy amount for the new EQC set by the central government is KRW 6.3 million, while additional subsidies can be granted by local governments depending on where the car is registered.
