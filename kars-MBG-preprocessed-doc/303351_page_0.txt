Mercedes-Benz Korea officially launches plug-in hybrid SUVs,?The new Mercedes-Benz GLC 300 e 4MATIC and GLC 300 e 4MATIC Coup??- ?The new GLC 300 e 4MATIC? and ?The new GLC 300 e 4MATIC Coup?? are plug-in hybrid variants of the facelifted model of the best-selling mid-size SUVs the GLC and the GLC Coup?, and have been officially released in Korea- Both models feature an enhanced third-generation plug-in hybrid system, improved all-electric range with shorter charging time compared to the previous model, and a more powerful electric motor with superior efficiency and performance- To emphasize the brand?s promise in quality, Mercedes-Benz Korea is offering a complimentary and exclusive ?Warranty Plus? service for plug-in hybrid customers, which is an attractive benefit to support them with effective vehicle management through the extended warranty period of 5 years or 150,000km (whichever comes first), more than the existing Warranty Plus(Seoul, October 21, 2020) ? Mercedes-Benz Korea(Interim President & CEO Jee-seop Kim) announced on October 21 that the company has officially launched the mid-size plug-in hybrid SUVs ?The new Mercedes-Benz GLC 300 e 4MATIC? and ?The new Mercedes-Benz GLC 300 e 4MATIC Coup??, equipped with the third-generation plug-in hybrid technology that boasts superior performance and efficiency.The new GLC 300 e 4MATIC and the new GLC 300 e 4MATIC Coup? are the plug-in hybrid models of the GLC family, which has become the best-selling mid-size SUV after the facelifted models of the GLC and the GLC Coup? recorded sales of over 4,500 units since it was officially launched in Korea on January.With the launch of the plug-in hybrid models of the new GLC and the GLC Coup?, Mercedes-Benz Korea is continuing to strengthen its EQ brand lineup and expanding choices for customers. The EQ brand portfolio consists of various lineups such as: the ▲EQ Boost model with a 48V mild hybrid system; ▲EQ Power plug-in hybrid model, which allows drivers to select between an internal combustion engine and an electric motor; and a ▲full-electric model such as the new EQC, which was released in Korea on October 2019.Mercedes-Benz Korea Vice President of Product & Marketing Mark Raine said, ?As promised at the Annual Press Conference held earlier this year, we are pleased to present to our Korean customers a new eco-friendly powertrain variant of the new GLC and the new GLC Coup?, which includes the latest plug-in hybrid technology from Mercedes-Benz,? and that ?We will continue to introduce various EQ Boost and EQ Power lineup along with full-electric models to create a diverse EQ portfolio showcasing the future of electrification from Mercedes-Benz.?Hybrid powertrain with a more powerful and efficient engine compared to its predecessorThe new GLC 300 e 4MATIC and the new GLC 300 e 4MATIC Coup? are equipped with the third-generation plug-in hybrid technology from Mercedes-Benz, boasting an improved all-electric range and battery efficiency compared to the previous generation model.The hybrid powertrains of both models consist of an in-line 4-cylinder petrol engine with a maximum output and torque of 211hp and 35.7kg.m combined with an electric motor that delivers a further output and torque of 122hp and 44.9kg.m, thereby boasting a maximum system output of nearly 320hp. Both models are also equipped with the 9G-TRONIC automatic transmission exclusive to hybrid models and designed for the electric motor. A torque converter located between electric motor and transmission ensures smooth gear changes and improves energy efficiency.In addition, the capacity of the lithium-ion battery pack has been increased greatly from 8.7kWh in the previous plug-in hybrid system to 13.5kWh, resulting in a greater all-electric range of 25km by Korean testing standards (50km by NEDC) that provides a more satisfying electric driving experience.The new GLC 300 e 4MATIC and the new GLC 300 e 4MATIC Coup? come with EQ POWER-exclusive operating modes that provide the best driving system for the current driving situation, whether it is by hybrid, the electric motor or the engine. Drivers can select from four operating modes including ?Hybrid?, ?E-Mode?, ?E-Save?, and ?Charge? based on their preferences and driving conditions. Operating modes can be selected using either switches or buttons, or left for the system to decide automatically.The new GLC 300 e 4MATIC and the new GLC 300 e 4MATIC Coup? come with the DYMATIC SELECT which provides 6 different driving modes to match your personal driving style. For the Plug-in hybrids exclusive modes, ?Battery Level? and ?Electric?, are provided in addition to the existing 4 driving modes: ?Eco?, ?Comfort?, ?Sport?, and ?Individual?. Under the ?Battery Level? mode, the vehicle limits the use of electric consumption by using the petrol engine and maintains the battery charge at a certain level to prepare for all-electric driving in the future. While with the ?Electric? mode, the vehicle runs on the electric motor alone, creating a virtual engine sound through a sound generator. The Electric mode can be activated using the paddle shifts located on the steering wheel.In addition, the haptic accelerator pedal informs the driver when to release the accelerator and sends a vibrating signal when the engine can be used, allowing for an improved driving efficiency.Advanced driving systems and convenience features that support comfortable driving The new GLC 300 e 4MATIC and the new GLC 300 e 4MATIC Coup? also offer various safety and convenience features as standard to provide drivers with a safer and more pleasant driving environment.Both models come with the Driving Assistance Package as a standard feature, which helps to improve both safety and driver convenience. This includes the ▲Active Distance Assist DISTRONIC which helps to maintain distance with the vehicle in front and supports automatic speed adjustment, braking, and restarts, ▲Active Brake Assist with Cross Traffic function, ▲Active Lane Keeping Assist, ▲exit warning function and PRE-SAFE® PLUS.Also, both models are equipped with the LED high performance headlamps as standard. Featuring a smoother design with its smaller size and contoured details, they are more efficient and brighter, offering a wider field of illumination while being able to automatically adjust its brightness based on the driving environment, which helps to reduce driver fatigue.Using the latest MBUX(Mercedes-Benz User Experience) infotainment system, drivers can also check the state of charge and the energy flow of the vehicle and easily operate various functions using the intelligent voice recognition, touch screen, touchpad, and control panels on the steering wheel.In addition, both models offer standard convenience features including: ▲Mercedes me connect, a premium EV service that allows drivers to check the state of charge and driving distance through the smartphone app; ▲Advanced sound system that provides a more dynamic sound; ▲KEYLESS GO® package; ▲sliding sunroof; ▲head-up display; ▲wireless charging support; and ▲a heated steering wheel.Upgraded, high-efficiency battery system and a reassuring warranty serviceThe new GLC 300 e 4MATIC and the new GLC 300 e 4MATIC Coup? are equipped with a high-efficiency battery system consisting of a 13.5kWh lithium-ion battery manufactured by Daimler subsidiary Deutsche ACCUMOTIVE. In addition, the capacity of the on-board charger has more than doubled from 3.6kW in the previous model to 7.4kW, reducing the charging time by half as the battery can be fully charged in 1 hour and 45 minutes using the Mercedes-Benz charger. In addition, Mercedes-Benz Korea offers a warranty for 8 years or 160,000km (whichever comes first) to the lithium-ion battery applied to the vehicle.Meanwhile, Mercedes-Benz Korea is also offering the plug-in hybrid exclusive version of the extended warranty program ?Warranty Plus? at no extra costs to customers who purchase the new GLC 300 e 4MATIC, the new GLC 300 e 4MATIC Coup? and select EQ Power plug-in hybrid models with longer service warranty than any other models to emphasize the brand?s pride in its quality. While the existing Warranty Plus benefit extends the service warranty to up to 5 years or 140,000km (whichever comes first), the plug-in hybrid exclusive Warranty Plus offers an additional warranty of 10,000km in addition to the existing Warranty Plus. With a total warranty of up to 5 years or 150,000km (whichever comes first), this exclusive customer benefit further supports efficient vehicle management.The new GLC 300 e 4MATIC and the new GLC 300 e 4MATIC Coup? are available at KRW 75,800,000 and KRW 79,900,000 respectively, with VAT included.
